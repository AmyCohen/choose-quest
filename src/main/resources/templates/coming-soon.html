Coming Soon!

Little Quest is constantly under improvement and evolving to add new and better features. Below are some features and attractions that are in-process. Look out for updates!

  • New stories

    • Looking for new content? No problem! New stories are in progress and will be added when complete.

  • Create your own stories

    • Do you have an idea for a questing adventure? We'd love to provide a platform for you to express yourself. We're working on a way for you to create your story and have it added to our library so others can experience your wit and creativity.

  • Reading levels

    • Would you like to choose stories faster by having access to the reading level associated with the story? We would too! A feature is going to be added for filtering by reading level to provide you with the best curated library.

  • Reading times

    • Only have a limited time but really want to go on an adventure? Choose by reading time! At least you will be able to filter for reading times soon.

  • User progress

    • Dislike having to start stories over from scratch? We do too! Soon you'll be able to save your progress in a story and be able to pick back up where you left off. No more walking the same path over and over to get to your destination!

  • AND MORE!

    • Little Quest is not done and will likely be evolving for a while as we come up with more and new things to make adventures more fun and accessible.
